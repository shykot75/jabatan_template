XPATS GATEWAY - CENTRALISED PLATFORM FOR EXPATRIATES' SUPPORT LETTER APPLICATION FROM AGENCIES

31 July 2023


Greetings from MYXpats Centre!


Referring to our announcement on 1 June 2023, the Xpats Gateway, officially launched on 15 June 2023, represents the Government's commitment to digitising governance while ensuring that Malaysia remains a business-friendly country that supports its economic and investment goals.


We are pleased to announce that effective 31 July 2023 onwards, the Xpats Gateway system has been extended to include more sectors. Employers under the purview of the following Agencies can now apply a Support Letter for their expatriate hiring through Xpats Gateway. The Xpats Gateway can be accessed via the ESD Online at esd.imi.gov.my.

Upon obtaining a letter from the respective agencies, the employer can continue with the application for the final decision by the Immigration Department of Malaysia via the ESD Online (esd.imi.gov.my).

For sectors not listed above, employers may obtain their Support Letters directly from respective Agencies as usual until the full implementation of Xpats Gateway.


Note: For other sectors not under the purview of Agencies, the employers can apply their expatriate applications directly to ESD Online (esd.imi.gov.my).

However, the Expatriate Committee or the Immigration Department of Malaysia reserves the right to request additional information to support the decision-making.
